Business and Consulting Club, IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi's premier undergrad business + consulting club.

BnC focuses on organizing events and projects likehosting industry experts and startup founders through seminars, organizing case-competitions, conducting interview bootcamps and providing consulting services to startups/organizations. We provide opportunities for skill building along with creativity, overall making this club an inclusive space for personal as well as collective growth.

What we do

Flagship Events

+

The Events Vertical at BnC is where the excitement happens — a space designed to push your boundaries, test your creativity, and give you a taste of real-world business in fun and competitive formats. From fast-paced challenges to high-stakes simulations and inspiring speaker sessions, this is where you get to learn, compete, and grow — all while having a great time with your peers.

  • Guestimate League – Crack fun, fast-paced guesstimation rounds.
  • Mystery Business Challenge – Solve quirky, unpredictable business puzzles.
  • CXO Talks – Learn straight from industry leaders who’ve built and scaled businesses.
  • Startup Expo Case Competition – Compete with ideas, showcase startups, and celebrate innovation

Corporate Comms

+

The vertical connects the club with consulting firms, VCs, companies, and consulting bodies of other institutes, creating collaborations that enrich our activities

  • The team reaches out to companies for live projects, workshops, and speaker sessions to give students real-world exposure
  • We also manage communication, publicity, and the club’s representation, ensuring BnC maintains a strong and credible presence in and outside the campus.

Startup Consulting

+

We partner with early-stage ventures to address strategic and operational challenges.

  • Through real projects, members gain hands-on experience in market analysis, business models, and growth strategies while making tangible impact
